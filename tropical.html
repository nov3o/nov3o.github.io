Tropical Algebra

Tropical Algebra, also known as Max-Plus Algebra, is a mathematical framework that replaces traditional arithmetic operations with max and addition operations. It is a powerful algebraic structure with various applications in different fields.

Operations

In Tropical Algebra, addition takes the place of traditional multiplication, and the maximum operation replaces addition. This unique approach has intriguing properties, such as idempotence and zero element properties.

Applications

Tropical Algebra finds applications in a wide range of domains. It is used in optimization problems, control theory, and machine learning.
